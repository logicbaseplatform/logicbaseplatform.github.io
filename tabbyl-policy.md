# Privacy Policy for Tabbyl

**Effective Date:** 28 January 2026

Tabbyl (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the Tabbyl mobile application (“the App”), which is a point-of-sale (POS) solution for cafés and restaurants. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use Tabbyl.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### a. Information You Provide
We may collect information that you voluntarily provide, such as:
- Business name and basic profile details
- User account details (such as name or role)
- Order, billing, and table data entered into the App

### b. Automatically Collected Information
When you use the App, we may automatically collect:
- Device information (device type, operating system version)
- App usage data (screens visited, actions performed)
- Crash logs and performance data

This data is used only to improve app stability and performance.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and operate the Tabbyl POS features
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Monitor performance and fix bugs
- Provide customer support when requested

We do **not** use your data for advertising or marketing purposes.

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

Tabbyl does **not** sell, trade, or rent your personal or business data to third parties.

We may share limited data only:
- When required by law or legal process

- With trusted service providers strictly for app operation (e.g., crash reporting), under confidentiality obligations

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide the App’s functionality or to comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy

Tabbyl is intended for use by businesses and is **not designed for children under the age of 13**. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
